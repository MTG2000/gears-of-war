The Story so far ....

A new saga begins for one of the most acclaimed video game franchises in history. After narrowly escaping an attack on their village, JD Fenix and his friends, Kait and Del, must rescue the ones they love and discover the source of a monstrous new enemy. 

Meet The New Generation of soldiers

Twenty five years have passed since the decimation of the Locust & Lambent threats on the planet Sera. However a new race of monsters have taken their place and JD Fenix, son of series protagonist Marcus Fenix, has taken up the mantle in helping to protect humanity.

Fast Paced Gameplay

The Game feature a quick and fast paced action where every move counts. Get Ready to test your reaction time and your skills alone or with your friends to fight against the hordes of monstrous aliens.

Competitve Multiplayer

The development for Multiplayer began at the same time as Campaign so there was an equal focus on both aspects of the game. Horde also returned with the inclusion of combat classes and skill and ability progression.
